Is insurance necessary ?

 

Health insurance has become an important part when you live in every country. Moving to Finland, it is also very necessary to take the insurance, because with foreigners, the cost in Finland is truly high. 

Some people still concern whether they should buy it, because buying the insurance, you have to pay a considerable amount at first.

This article will introduce you to the benefits of buying insurance and how it works in Finland with the hope that you will have a clear sight and make the best decision.

 

 

1) Is insurance necessary ?

 

Being a foreigner to Finland, especially foreign students, life here is not easy at all due to the high living cost. Whenever you get in trouble with your health or accident, the expense for curing is not so cheap. For that, insurance is very necessary to cover a part of the cost for you. It includes the medical and drug expenses.

The insurance will be provided based on your duration of studying or working in Finland. 

In recent years, insurance has been the compulsory document to complete the Application of Visa in some countries. If not, it is also your advantages to have an insurance to easier pass the Application of Visa

With residents of EU/EEA that have the European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) before,  you don’t need to take out the insurance again. All you need to do is attach the certification of insurance or a copy of your card to the application for Insurance in Finland.

The insurance can be taken with with the resident permit, with the following information

  • personal details
  • period of validity of insurance
  • geographical area in which your insurance is valid
  • insurance coverage
  • insured amounts
  • amount of excess, or a mention that there is no excess

 

 

2) Kind of insurance in Finland

 

All insurance in Finland must meet the following requirements:

The cost of the insurance must be less than €300

Less than 1 year: you have to make sure that your insurance is valid during the entire stay in Finland.t More than 1 year: with people those study in Finland in a long time, your insurance must be available without a break for at least one year and cover the entire time you stay there

*Note: If the valid time of the insurance is less than the time you apply for residence permit, you must be careful to renew it when it is going to be out of date.

If you are studying in a short time, the expanse insurance coverage you are taking you need to be more extensive. It means that if you are an exchange student, you need more coverage than a degree student in Finland.

Travel insurance: only valid during the trips with the maximum of 90 days.

Your insurance must me valid when you are in Finland

 

 

3) Apply for insurance in Finland

 

You can take out insurance from your country insurance company. You have to search for the suitable and reliable company as well as the international insurance company to support your insurance in Finland.

Here are some international health insurance companies for students that you can contact:

  • ACS: French company Assurances Courtages et Services (www.acs-ami.com)
  • AON Students Insurance: family company of the AON Corporation (www.students-insurance.eu)
  • The US company International Student Insurance (www.internationalstudentinsurance.com)
  • ESI Finland: Insurance company Swisscare  (https://swisscare.com/)
  • SIP Integral (https://www.sipinsurance.eu)

 

 

You can have a research here for further information: https://migri.fi/en/insurance 

 

Life in Finland is not as difficult as you think, as you don’t need to worry much about the accident and medical issue if you have the insurance. Obviously seeing the importance of insurance, many countries nowadays make insurance become a compulsory document, so why don’t you take one for yourself when in other countries ? If you need more information, don’t hesitate to click on “SWISSCARE WITH YOU” and  “MEDICAL PROBLEMS IN FINLAND”
