Top reputable Travel Agencies in Vietnam
For travelling around the EU, especially Finland


  1. Travelling around the EU is now common with people, especially with the Vietnamese. They choose the EU because when they come to a random nation in the EU, they can easily go to others without any significant problems.
  2. Besides some popular countries such as German, Italy, England, many Vietnamese people also have an interest in exploring Finland, a country with few people and gorgeous nature scenes.
  3. However, there are some groups of people still wondering about how much reputation a travel company can bring them, how can they make them ensure about their life, money and their trip. In this blog, we will introduce for you - travellers to some reputable travel companies in Vietnam whose strength is the EU, including Finland.

1. There are many reasons to explain why people choose the EU for their vacation and free time.

a. Learn about the history of ancient civilizations: Civilization in Europe has been around for a long time and this continent has buildings to confirm that, especially in places like Rome that can help you learn about history in the most practical way. These cities are growing, but they always remind of a precious past with lots of true stories of history just waiting for you to discover.

b. European cuisine has many varieties: Cuisine is also one of the features of Europe. Many famous dishes are derived from here. You must have heard of the Michelin award already, right. This is one of the most prestigious awards in the world for culinary excellence for outstanding restaurants and chefs. Notably, Europe is also one of the places with the most 3-star Michelin restaurants globally. You can enjoy top-of-the-line cuisines prepared by people with expertise in the field.

c. Europe's climate is very pleasant: With a full range of seasons: Spring - Summer - Fall - Winter create favourable conditions for tourism. If you travel to Europe in spring, you will be immersed in various special festivals, enjoying the culture of each local area. If you choose summer as the time to travel, the blue beaches, white sand are one of the must-do places in your journey. If you want to see the romantic yellow leaf streets, autumn is the right time. With the cool air with a little chilly plus the path full of golden colour will be an ideal time to relax.

d. Europe has a safe, innovative and better management system than many other countries in the world: When you come to the EU, you will feel comfortable when you live and enjoy your time there. It will bring you a precious moment with your loved ones without any caring about safe or risks. Neat, clean and planned streets are also a reason why people are likely to travel to Europe. 

 

2. Nowadays, Finland also a good choice for international visitors, including Vietnamese. Interesting facts about Finland, maybe those ones are a good point in attracting travellers

    1. Land of thousands of lakes: 187,888 is the exact number of lakes (more than 500 square meters) and 179,584 large and small islands in the Finnish territory and is considered one of the countries with the most lakes and islands in the world.
    2. The world's largest educational exotic system: Finland education system is rated as the best in the world with the "weirdest" that are: no exam, no teaching too much knowledge, no memorization, no fee, no need for a teacher. 
    3. Drink the most coffee: Besides alcohol, the Finns also love to drink coffee. This is one of the countries that consume the most coffee in the world. On average, each user consumes 12 kg of pure coffee a year, while this figure in Italy is 5.7 kg and in Spain is 4.5 kg. There is the fact that Vietnam is one of the biggest countries in exporting coffee to other countries and regions around the world.
