Top 10 Finnish scholarships
for International students in 2021

 

It is not hard at all for international students to obtain a scholarship in Finland but there is more to it than just doing a “search on Google”.

The most common hurdle for bachelor's and master's degree students in Finland is the lack of accurate and up-to-date information. That is why at FinInNote we are providing foreign English Speaking students - who would like to study abroad in The Happiest Country in the world, with the latest study facts and a piece of mind.

This article will be providing guidelines for how to find a scholarship for Finland Universities and How to apply for them online without paying a hefty sum for a study agency - which can be another big mistake that students in Asia often make.

While we are at it - We will also show you the list of Top scholarships in Finland for all International students who need to pay the tuition fee and their actual requirements. So please stay tuned and read on.

 

1. Scholarship in Finland:

a. What you need to know:

There are two types of school that provide higher education in Finland:

University

  • Offer more “researcher” study curriculum which students are trained to be researchers and will have the choice to pursue higher education such as Doctoral degrees
  • Will be the only type of University that sometimes provides full scholarships plus  money for living

University Of Applied Science (UAS)

  • Offer a more “practical” approach in which students will be taught in the mindset of an Entrepreneur and will have a higher chance to get a job offer after graduation. Including Bachelor and Master Degrees
  • Will be providing a 50% scholarship if students complete at least 60ETCs per year.

c. To sum up: 

  • There are not as many scholarships for bachelor degree students as there are for master degrees in English.
  • All International students will automatically be eligible for 50% scholarship as long as they complete their study with 60ECTS per academic year - it is already a lot cheaper than the tuition fee in the USA and Canada
  • UAS will be more practical and provide more support for job-seeking students after graduation.
  • University will provide the most talented foreign Students with 100% scholarship and some cost of living if they are selected and complete 55 - 60 ECTS per academic year.

 

2. How to find and apply for a scholarship for English speaking International Students?

a. There are two places where you can hunt for a Finland scholarship

  1. On the Official University Websites
  2. The scholarship directories:
    • Scholarship Portals
    • Scholarship Plus

DISCLAIMER: We are NOT in any way affiliated with the sites provided above and we do not make any profit in advertising for any programs in this post.

 

b. How to apply for a scholarship in Finland

By 2020 all Universities and UAS in Finland will only accept the application for a scholarship together with the admission application before the semester starts.

  • Look for the appropriate scholarships
  • Decide which schools are best for your study
  • Note down the requirements and time of admission
  • Apply for a scholarship together with the admission application

 

 

Getting a scholarship for international students in Finland wasn’t so hard after all. You just need to follow our guidelines and be prepared for all the requirements that the school requests. It is already super easy to get a 50% scholarship and most of the tuition fees are considered a lot cheaper than other countries.
