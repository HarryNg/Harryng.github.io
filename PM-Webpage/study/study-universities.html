WORTH TAKING RESIDENCE PERMIT BY “YOU”

 

The residence permit is one of the must-have files to complete migration in other countries. For its important essence, taking a residence permit is very complicated and confuses people whether they are doing it correctly or what they should do with plenty of necessary papers.

That’s the reason why there are many education agencies nowadays with strong support and detailed instructions for you to complete your process easily and safely. All you need to do is pay for their service at a high price. But with many foreign students who are currently living in Finland, consider that it is unworthy

Let’s get why they think so and try to do it by ourselves!

 

1) Why is taking RP through an agency is unworthy?

First, all the information can be easily found on the Finland website at www.migi.fi with the RP suitable types and instructions of the process. This website is official to follow.

Second, what the agency does is just translate from English to Vietnamese and tell us to do it. They are not able to authenticate your personal document, they are not able to do the fingerprints for you, and so on. You may think they make a big effort in your process, but actually not.

Taking RP by yourself is also a challenge for you at first. When you live in another country, you have to face lots of new information written in English and you have to solve it alone. For that reason, according to many previous foreign students, they highly recommend us to do it by ourselves.


2) How can we do it by ourselves? 

Applying for RP in Finland can be briefly described in 6 steps:

  • Make sure that you meet the requirements of RP
  • Make sure that all the attachments are up-to-date, translated, and legalize
  • Fill in the RP application 
  • Pay for your service
  • Appointment of Finnish Immigration Service
  • Wait for the result (If there is anything necessary, they will contact you. If not, don’t worry because the process may be different and also the result announcing time).

3) Video instruction 

You can follow the instructions in this video to carry out your own application.

Although maybe this is your first time to overcome such difficult work, be confident at first. If you don’t believe in yourself, the front time will be harder and more challenging. Also, applying by yourself can reduce a huge amount of studying abroad budget. In the meantime, you can also read our “ FIRST TIME TO FINLAND”.
